Founding Principles.

01

Aligned Incentives

The network sustains itself through membership, not by taxing the deals it facilitates. When the incentive is to make the network more valuable — not to extract more from it — everything works.

02

Infrastructure Over Noise

No forums. No events. No content. EARN exists to route referrals and document agreements. Everything else was deliberately left out.

03

Scarcity by Design

Most networks add agents until the network is worthless. EARN did the opposite. One agent per territory, no exceptions. Scarcity is not a limitation — it protects the monopoly.
